Short story

My full name is Thomas Bertrand-Batlle and I'm a CGI artist.
I've been working freelance since I graduated from Supinfocom Arles in 2012 and am a member of Bandits Collective.

I've been working in London, Paris and Hong Kong for advertising and fashion studios, directors, architects and developers.

I'm now living in France, being a lecturer and mentor at MoPA in Arles, France and doing commissioned work worldwide.
