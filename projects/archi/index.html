What we do

After graduation, David Calvet, Bastien Letoile, Julien Soler and I created Bandits Collective, a formation of animation directors and artists who collaborate on commissioned work or self-funded short films.

I'm involved in this collective as director, art director, editor and scenarist. These roles rotate pending projects.

Our latest project "The Little Things" got selected in festivals across Europe and was finialist in "Best Expanded Animation" at the British Animation Awards.
