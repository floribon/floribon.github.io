What I do

London based for the last 7 years, I've worked freelance across projects in advertising, fashion, interactive and architecture, using CG, realtime, VR and AR.

After graduating from SUPINFOCOM Arles (MoPA) in France, I later came to specialize in lighting and rendering; for both photo-realistic and non photo-realistic (NPR) setups.
