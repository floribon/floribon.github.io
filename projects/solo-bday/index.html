What I teach

I'm involved as a speaker and professeur at MoPA (formerly Supinfocom) in France and The Animation Workshop in Denmark.

From introducing the student artists to their first CG projects to mentor their graduation films, I follow their journey through the art of animation. Either very technical or art focused, my lessons cover the visual development, lighting and rendering.
